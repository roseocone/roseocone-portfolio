About Me

I'm Rose Ocone, a recent film school graduate with a passion for visual storytelling through film and photography. My work explores narrative, emotion, and the human experience captured through the lens.

As a filmmaker and director, I specialize in creating compelling short films that challenge perspectives and engage audiences. My artistic practice combines cinematography, directing, and creative vision to bring stories to life. I work as a 1st and 2nd AD on productions, collaborating with directors to bring their vision to the screen with precision and creativity.

I'm deeply experienced in casting, working as a casting director on multiple film projects. I believe the right actor can transform a character and elevate an entire production. I'm passionate about discovering new talent and connecting compelling characters with their perfect actors to bring scripts to life authentically.

Casting & Talent

Connecting storytellers with exceptional talent

Casting Philosophy

I believe the right actor can transform a character and elevate an entire production. My approach to casting focuses on finding authentic performances that resonate with the story's emotional core and enhance the overall narrative.

Experience

As a recent film school graduate, I've worked as a casting director on multiple student films and independent projects including Static Windows, Un Poco de Mexico, Scaredy Driver, The Ambersons, and Decision Day. I'm passionate about discovering new talent and collaborating with actors to bring scripts to life.
