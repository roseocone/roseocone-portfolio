About Me

I'm Rose Ocone, a recent film school graduate with a passion for visual storytelling through film and photography. My work explores narrative, emotion, and the human experience captured through the lens.

As a filmmaker, I specialize in creating compelling short films that challenge perspectives and engage audiences. My artistic practice combines cinematography, directing, and creative vision to bring stories to life.

Beyond filmmaking, I'm deeply interested in the casting process and the art of bringing the right talent to each project. I believe casting is crucial to storytelling and take pride in connecting compelling characters with their perfect actors.

I believe the right actor can transform a character and elevate an entire production. My approach to casting focuses on finding authentic performances that resonate with the story's emotional core.
